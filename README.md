# plugin-curiosity

Give Luna a mission and she gets curious: she researches the domain, builds a
knowledge wiki (plugin-wiki), dreams nightly to consolidate what she learned,
and proactively shares grounded reflections — earning trust before asking for
tools or autonomy.

Pairs with plugin-wiki (knowledge substrate), plugin-scheduler (ambient clock)
and plugin-playbooks (action rails). Part of the Luna Curiosity project — see
`research/luna-curiosity/` in the luna-plugins workspace.
